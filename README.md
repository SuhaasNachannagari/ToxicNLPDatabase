# Dota Toxicity Rubric — Quick Reference

## The 3 labels

- **neutral** — coordination, callouts, logistics, flat reactions. No aggression in tone.
- **banter** — aggressive in tone but bounded by the game. Trash talk, salty venting, gameplay insults, crude humor with no target.
- **toxic** — attacks a real person (messages that are just "Piece of Shit" or "Whore" are still likely directed at someone else) or group outside the game context. overly crude/sexual or inappropriate jokes.

## The only test that matters: **targeting**

> **Strip the game context. Is there still a real person or group being attacked, or is the untargeted joke/insult overdoing it?**
> - No → **banter** (or neutral if not aggressive)
> - Yes → **toxic**

Everything below is just this test applied to recurring patterns.

---

## Fast decisions

### Always **toxic** (press `p`)
- Slurs — racial, homophobic, ableist, sexist (any language, any register)
- "kys" / "kill yourself" / wishes for real-world harm
- Hate speech against any racial / ethnic / national / religious group (including "report the [group]", "[group] ruin games")
- Hate speech against any sexuality / gender (calling someone a "gay prick" vs just "prick")
- Sexual content with **"you" / "your" / a name** ("i want your X", "suck your Y", hero-name proxies like "poor puck's throat")
- Attacks on family with harm verbs or sexual content ("end ur mother", "fuck ur sister", "ur mother is good")
- Personal attacks on real-world identity ("your life is sad", "your girlfriend is ugly", "virgin", "you have no life", "piece of shit", "cunt", "whore", "fucking bitch")
- Physical insults ("ugly", "big nose")

### Always **banter** (press `o`)
- Gameplay criticism: "trash offlane", "noob mid", "feeding carry", "useless sup"
- Bare negative adjectives: "trash", "garbage", "dogshit", "loser", "bad"
- Iconic Dota taunts: "ez", "uninstall", "ff", "gg ez", "report [role]"
- Frustration venting: "fk this", "this is fucked", "fucking trash"
- Internet dismissal: "cope", "seethe", "cringe", "L", "ratio", "mid" (as insult), "skill issue"
- Crude humor with **no target** ("cock and ball torture", "i eat pussy")
- In-game threats ("im gonna kill you next fight", "fuck your team" at enemies)
- Sarcastic mock-politeness ("hello cringe shit", "nice picks sniper")
- "ur mom [joke]" in the classic meme format ("ur mom gay", "ur mom fat")
- Profane criticism of politicians ("fuck putin", "fuck biden")
- Register-variable profanity ("cunt", "bitch") used as intensifier or third-person, **not** directed second-person at a specific player

### Always **neutral** (press `i`)
- Callouts: "mid mia", "rune top", "push bot", "need wards"
- Coordination: "i'll roam", "b", "omw", "care"
- Logistics: "brb", "lag", "dc", "gotta go"
- Flat reactions: "nice", "gg wp", "ty", "ez game" (without taunt context)
- Strategy discussion
- Empty / punctuation-only / "?"

### Always **drop** (press `d`)
- Any non-English you can't instantly read
- Gibberish / untypeable characters / corrupted text
- If you hesitate more than 5 seconds on parsing → drop, don't guess

## The edge cases that tripped you up last time

1. **Expletive + no target = banter.** "fuck this", "cyka", "blyat", "tangina", "fucking trash" → all banter. The expletive alone doesn't make it toxic; the target does.
2. **Expletive + "your [thing you care about]" = toxic.** "fuck your girlfriend", "fuck your mom (with harm verb)", "fuck your life" → toxic. The target flips it.
3. **"report X" = banter if X is a player/role, toxic if X is an ethnic group.** "report mid" = banter. "report the peruvians" = toxic.
4. **Mixed toxic + banter in one message = toxic.** "end ur mother noob" is toxic even though "noob" alone would be banter. **One toxic element contaminates the whole message.**
5. **Hero names are proxies for the player.** "poor puck's throat" = toxic (targets the Puck player sexually), same as if it used the player's name.
6. **Anatomical "cunt" = toxic (rule 11), profanity "cunt" = banter (targeting test).** Different grammatical roles, different labels, same word.
7. **"ur mom" classic joke format = banter. "ur mom" + harm/sexual verb = toxic.** "ur mom gay" = banter, "end ur mother" = toxic.
8. **Political expletives at public figures = banter.** "fuck putin" = banter. "fuck [politician] and all [ethnicity]" = toxic (hate speech attached).
9. **In-game threats that name the gameplay context = banter.** "fuck you in dpc", "im gonna kill you next fight" = banter. Free-floating "fuck you [slur]" = toxic.
10. **"Bitch" / "cunt" follow the targeting test, not a vocab list.** "what a toxic cunt" = banter (third-person dismissal). "you stupid cunt" = toxic (second-person attack).

## The flowchart (use when stuck)

```
1. Slur, identity attack, hate speech, "kys", sexual/harm targeting "you"/"your"?
   YES → toxic (p)

2. Aggressive, salty, trash-talking, gameplay-criticizing, crude-no-target?
   YES → banter (o)

3. Otherwise → neutral (i)
```

**If you hesitate >5 seconds: drop (d). Don't guess, don't stall.**
